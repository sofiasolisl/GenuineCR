About us

This is Genuine!

Genuine Costa Rica was born from a family dream originally known as Bejuco Authentic in the Bejuco district of Nandayure. In 2018, we made the decision to relocate from the Islita community, situated south of the province of Guanacaste, to Liberia in the northern part of the province, in pursuit of new job and study opportunities.The revival of Bejuco Authentic was always ingrained in our family ventures. In 2020, we took on the management of Eco Camping Papagayo, a camping site and restaurant located on Panamá Beach. Serving as the launchpad, Bejuco Authentic and Eco Camping paved the way for the inception of Genuine Costa Rica in 2024.

Our purpose is to generate genuine connections through contact with Costa Rican forests, wildlife, oceans, local culture, cuisine, and our certified guides, crafting experiences that leave a lasting impression.

The founders

Marco Alvarado

Marco is a holder of a Master's degree in Sustainable Tourism from the National University in Costa Rica, boasting 15 years of expertise within the realm of 5-star hospitality companies. His recent position in hospitality spanned over three years, where he served as the manager of water sports operations. Certified as a Naturalist Guide by the National Institute of Learning, Marco, in response to the pandemic in 2020, reimagined his business and launched Eco Camping Papagayo, a camping site and restaurant. In 2023, he successfully brought the revitalized Genuine Costa Rica project to fruition.

Vielka Urbina

Vielka is a graduate in Industrial Engineering from the University of Costa Rica, with over 15 years of experience in 5-star hotel companies in areas such as quality, operations, room division, and sustainability certifications like CST, Travelife, ISO 50001. Together, we are in charge of the operation of Genuine Costa Rica, working diligently on our commitment to safety, quality, responsibility, and service with all our clients and business partners.
